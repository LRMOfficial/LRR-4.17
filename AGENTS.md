# Life Row Records - Agent Instructions

## Voice and Tone
- **Branding**: The brand uses "Life Row Records" with fixed styling and uppercase headings ending in "//".
- **Tone**: The application should maintain a professional, serious, and high-fidelity tone. Avoid overly casual language or "vibe" slang in official sections (like Privacy or Contact).
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, high-contrast (Black/White/Brand Red/Brand Blue), utilizing `motion` for smooth, purposeful animations.

## Privacy & Security
- **Data Protection**: Life Row Records has a zero-sharing policy for user data. Emails and phone numbers MUST NOT be shared with third parties.
- **Privacy Policy**: All legal documents must be written in a formal, professional tone with clear section numbering.

## Frontend Standards
- **Icons**: Use `lucide-react` exclusively.
- **Animations**: Use `motion/react` for all entrance and interaction effects.
- **Layout**: Desktop-first precision with mobile-responsive code (Tailwind CSS).
